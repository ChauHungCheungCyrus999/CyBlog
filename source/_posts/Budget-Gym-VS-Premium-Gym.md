# Budget Gym or Premium Gym?
<p>I have seen endless debate from day one - “Budget Gym or Premium Gym”  Here’s my honest take from real experience!</p>
## Introduction
<p>My gym journey kicked off with LCSD gyms, moved to CityU’s facilities, and leveled up to EFX24 in Tsuen Wan.  I see the gym from the LCSD and CityU one as Budget Gym and the EFX24 one as premium gym based on the equirement number and the price. I am not here to persuade you to pick any of them. Just sharing insights of it</p>
## The experience of Budget Gym
<p>Friends kept recommending budget spots when I started hunting for a gym. A 24/7 chain’s one-day trial tempted me, but $550 monthly? Too steep for a broke F6 student unsure if I’d stick with it—plenty buy memberships and ghost after months. LCSD gyms won out eventually!</p>
<p>Tsuen Wan Sports Centre became my spot: benches, cables, machines for all body parts. Pure fun from February 2023. CityU’s setup improved on it—bench press benches, squat racks—where I started building chest fundamentals. It will be wonderful for low-budget starters testing if gym life clicks.</p>
<img src="/images/IMG_20230206_154127.jpg"  alt="Tsuen Wan LCSD(First day going to the gym)" width="200"/>
## The experience of Premium Gym
<p>By late 2025e Sem A, my progress stalled. Faster gains meant mastering form via YouTube/coaches or upgrading equipment for easier training. I skipped YouTube time-sucks (lateral raises, back moves) and coach fees, picking better gear instead. Since switching to permium gum, those machine help me target my muscle groups better. I can see a big difference on that,</p>
## Benefits of Budget Gym
<ol>
<li>
<p>High Cost-Effectiveness<br />
For the LCSD gym, if you are student, its $90 for monthly ticket and $12 for a hour.<br />
For the CityU gym, its $3 for 50mins. its cheap<br />
Its very effortable for those whose family aint that wealthy and didnt want to put some portion of money into doing sport</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A Great Starting Point<br />
Budget gyms suit beginners without advanced skills. The low cost of quitting means minimal regret if it doesn’t click right away.</p>
</li>
</ol>
## Pros of Premium Gym
<ol>
<li>
<p>Low Skill Barrier<br />
It didnt require you to have a high level of skill and knowledge.<br />
Because if u want to train the target that u want using free weight/ dumbbell, it take you lots of skill for it. I know lots of ppl that work out in LCSD gym and their muscle mass is big and the whole body shape is shredded but most of them really use lots of time and experience on studying how to train the target muscle.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Superior Environment<br />
Premium gym provide a cleaner space for the user to use there. and it didnt require the gym member to take a long time for a machine. You can chat with your friend and have a comfortable gym session</p>
</li>
</ol>
## Whats my choice
<p>For me, I recommend other to go to budget gym if they unsure that committing long term. And if your technique aint like pure and want to have a faster progress, Premium gym can definitely evulate your game.</p>
<p>Gym memberships are the best investment I’ve made so far. They’ve rebuilt my confidence during tough times, help me build up a healthy lifestyle and sculpted my body shape. Hope everyone can give it a try. I promise it will give you worthy return.</p>
