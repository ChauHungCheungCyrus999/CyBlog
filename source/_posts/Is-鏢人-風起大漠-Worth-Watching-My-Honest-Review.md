# Is Blades of the Guardians(鏢人:風起大漠) Worth Watching? My Honest Review
<p>I want to keep this review real . I watched Blades of the Guardians(鏢人:風起大漠) while visiting Changle -my uncle bring aunt and me to theater. The teaser trailer looks very attractive and produced by Wu jing(a guy famous for epci wa and action films). So we gave it a shot!</p>
<img src="/images/鏢人.jpg"  alt="鏢人poster" width="600"/>
<p>I’ll break down the movie in the simplest way possible, there is a “2nd wanted criminal”&quot; who is also a “鏢人”(someone that do body guard or bounty hunter). Lil bro got a mission to escort the “top most wanted guy” to a place. The movie is about the journey of it.</p>
# My honest feeling of it
<p>I felt confusing when I watch this- different story part connected super unnaturally. I was having some feelings like “how this enemy suddenly beefing with lil bro?” “wheres this lil bro come from”.</p>
<p>The fighting scenes are very messy. My honest reaction: 🤔🤯😵😵‍💫. All the rapid angle switches made me dizzy. I low key perfer more the short “usagi vs random monsters” that generated by seedance2.0 more than the fighting scena in this movie.</p>
# Rating
<p>2/10- Those 2 marks is because i aint the one paying the ticket HAHAHAHAHAHA. Big thanks my uncle for the movie.</p>
<p>Its a dizzing mess overall. Not recommended at all. Save it for when you’re battling insomnia!😂🤣</p>
